About Buy Smart Auto Sales 365

Buy Smart Auto Sales 365 LLC was founded in 2021 by Demetrius. What began as a one‑person effort has grown from humble beginnings into a small, community-focused dealership based in South Elgin. Demetrius started the business with a practical mission: increase access to reliable, affordable vehicles for everyday people by finding good candidates at auctions and trusted local sources, repairing or refreshing them as needed, and offering them to buyers with clear information and fair pricing.

Our approach is hands-on. Vehicles are purchased, inspected, and prepared with attention to detail — mechanical checks, basic maintenance, and cosmetic improvements are performed so each car is safe and presentable. We prioritize transparency: every vehicle listing includes photos and a candid description of condition, and we encourage buyers to inspect cars themselves or bring a mechanic.

As a small operation, customer service is personal. You’ll often speak directly with Demetrius or a member of his team, and we aim to treat every customer with respect and honesty. Whether you’re shopping for a first car, replacing a daily driver, or helping a family member find reliable transportation, we take the time to answer questions and make sure you leave with a vehicle that suits your needs.

Our commitment to the community extends beyond sales — we strive to build relationships based on trust. If you have special requests (specific model searches, feature preferences, or timing constraints), let us know and we’ll do our best to help locate a suitable vehicle. We welcome feedback and use it to improve our sourcing and preparation process.

Visit us in South Elgin to see current inventory, or reach out via the Contact section here to arrange a viewing or test drive. We look forward to helping you find the right car at a price that makes sense.
